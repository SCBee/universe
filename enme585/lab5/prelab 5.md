## 3

## 4

Converting to a frequency (Hz)

$$
f = \frac{\omega}{2\pi} = 1.007 \text{ Hz}
$$

Then calculating the period

$$
T = \frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{1.007} = 0.993 \text{ s}
$$

## 5

$$

$$
T_{D} = -\frac{\phi T}{360^{o}} = - \frac{45^{o}(0.993)}{360^{o}} = 0.124 \text{ s}
